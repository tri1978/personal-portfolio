About Natalia Garay name

As a Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) with a background in providing direct patient care, I decided to transition into the field of web designing due to my passion for technology and creativity. The transition involved gaining new skills in coding, graphic design, and user experience, as well as familiarizing myself with web development tools and software. While my previous role focused on physical care, my current role in web designing allows me to utilize my problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and empathy to create user-friendly and visually appealing websites. The transition has been challenging yet rewarding, as I am now able to combine my passion for technology with my desire to create meaningful and accessible digital experiences for users.
